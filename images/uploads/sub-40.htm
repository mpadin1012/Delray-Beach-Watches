Certified is our new certification service, where verified watchmakers check and certify the authenticity of watches purchased on our marketplace. We want to provide buyers of luxury watches on Chrono24 with a completely worry-free purchasing experience.
1. Inspection by a verified watchmaker

Before the watch is listed, it is certified by a verified watchmaker who works with/for the dealer. As part of the certification process, an authenticity check and a function test are carried out, among other things. See also: How is the watch tested?

2. Look for listings with the dark blue Certified icon

You can identify Partner Certified watches by the dark blue “C” icon in the search results pages and respective listings. Of course, you can also use the filters to find Certified listings faster. Once you've opted for a Certified watch, you'll be able to see if it's Partner Certified or Chrono24 Certified on the corresponding listing page. See also: What’s the difference between Partner Certified and Chrono24 Certified?

3. Receive your certified watch including a digital certificate of authenticity

After successful certification, you can rest assured your new watch is genuine. This is confirmed by a digital Certificate of Authenticity and an authenticity guarantee. You can access the Certificate of Authenticity in your Chrono24 account after receiving the watch.

If you’re not satisfied, you can exercise your legal right of withdrawal and return your watch to the dealer within 14 days of receipt without giving a reason. If you purchased your watch from a private seller, we hold the purchase amount in our escrow account for 7 days after you have received your watch. Watches purchased through Partner Certified have already been certified and can be shipped directly to you.
When you purchase a watch through Partner Certified, certification is free for you. In this case, the watch has already been certified by a verified watchmaker working with/for the dealer. See also: What is the difference between Partner Certified and Chrono24 Certified?
You can identify Partner Certified watches by the dark blue Certified icon in the search results pages and respective listings. Since we’re constantly adding new watches to our Certified program, it’s worth periodically checking your favorite watches for the Certified icon.
All Certified watches are comprehensively checked against specific criteria by a verified watchmaker. This is the only way we can guarantee the authenticity of your watch. The check includes the following:

Authenticity check: The authenticity of the watch is checked both inside and out. We also check to make sure the watch meets our definition of authenticity. See: How do we define authenticity?

Functionality check: We test the watch's complications, winding mechanism, chronograph function, and more. See: How do we define authenticity?

Scope of delivery check: All additional items, such as the original box and papers (if included), are checked for completeness and conformity with the model reference.

Theft check: The watch's serial number is checked against leading stolen watch databases. This ensures that the watch hasn't been stolen or otherwise lost by its rightful owner.
Every seller who sells certified watches through Partner Certified is pre-screened using a set of strict criteria. In addition to meeting these criteria, we ensure that the seller has sufficient experience selling watches and can demonstrate a high level of customer satisfaction. Through targeted audits, we also ensure that every watchmaker working with our partners has all the necessary qualifications and technical equipment available. Should any shortcomings arise during the certification process, we will of course hold the respective watchmaker responsible.
The Certificate of Authenticity is a digital document that cannot be lost, stolen, or damaged. Once you have received the watch, you will find the certificate in your Chrono24 account. After logging in, you can access it either under the menu item "Buy" or in your Watch Collection.
Our general return conditions apply to the return of Certified watches. If you buy from a professional dealer, you can exercise your legal right of withdrawal within 14 days of receiving the watch and you’ll receive your money back quickly and easily.
